about

Found Media VJ Battle Royale (既有媒介VJ大战) is a browser-only audiovisual event that happened on 2026 April 26th, using publicly available internet videos as VJ sources. Hosted by [[rect*]] repair and UFO Terminal.
We split the venue in half and invited 24 VJs to battle head-to-head in two teams. Each round lasted 10 minutes, and the audience voted by physically standing in front of the VJ they enjoyed more.
The visual referneces spiraled across every corner of the internet. And because of the “browser only” rule, every VJ developed strange and highly improvised setups.
On this website we have asked every VJ to note down their creative process, thoughts and setup when participating in this event.
We love the internet 🌐🥺 We all love being online! This project might have a sequel ∠( ᐛ 」∠)


Found Media VJ Battle Royale（既有媒介VJ大战）是一场仅使用浏览器进行演出的视听活动，于2026年4月26日举办。所有VJ素材必须使用公开互联网视频，由 [[rect*]] repair 与 UFO Terminal 共同主办。
我们将整个场地一分为二，邀请24位VJ分成两队进行正面对决。每轮限时10分钟，观众通过实际站到（或者跳舞到）自己更喜欢的VJ前方进行投票。
整晚出现的视觉素材横跨互联网的每一个角落。而因为“只能使用浏览器”的规则，每位VJ都发展出了非常奇怪且高度即兴的演出方式与技术配置。
在这个网站中，我们邀请每位VJ记录下自己参与这场活动时的创作过程、想法与设备配置。
我们爱上网 🌐🥺！ 这个项目也许还会有续作 ∠( ᐛ 」∠)
